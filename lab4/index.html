Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

This lab is supposed to help us get into the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding. So we Pseudocode a simple computer game

Problems

Once we decided what game we wanted to do, we couldnt figure out how simple to make the instructions, or if we had to make it in a way that was eligible for JavaScript. So instead, we wrote out the steps on how to play the game and then step by step turned it into steps that JS could probably understand.
